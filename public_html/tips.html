Tips and Tricks for Dummies

Don't Forget To Use Up Your Resin

Original Resin replenishes every 8 minutes, so it is best to use them everyday to farm more efficiently.


Try To Save Your Fragile Resin For Artifact Farming

Fragile Resins that you get from doing quests and leveling adventure rank can be used to restore Original Resins. One Fragile Resin can restore 60 Original Resin. Try to save them up for late game.


Ley Lines Are Your Friends!

Whenever you find yourself lacking resources, remember that blue ley lines give EXP Books while gold ley lines give Mora.


Use The Adventurer's Handbook For Farming Enemy Drops

You can easily track enemies from the handbook. It will show you their location and possible drops. After killing a certain number of an enemy, you will have to wait for it to respawn again.


Raising Friendship Level

Growing 1 character will not only help you boost your level, but it will also increase your friendship level which will unlock more rewards like facial expressions.


Have At Least A Claymore & Bow User When Exploring

Not only elements, but also weapon types affect exploration & gathering. We recommend you to bring Claymore & Bow Users. Claymores: Claymores can break down ore for stone & metal resources while also dealing heavy damage to enemies with shields. Bows: There are also puzzles that require you to shoot things from a distance with a bow.


Don't Forget To Spend Your Sigils!

In order to purchase the items from the Souvenir Shop, you will need to collect and use Anemo Sigils as this is the main currency.


Try To Cook Food Whenever You Can!

Cooking can really be useful as it can create food that can heal, buff, and revive your characters! Be sure to use characters that give a special bonus when cooking.


Do Not Level Multiple Characters

As said before, only level up characters that you mostly use because resources can be hard to get later on. Also, most support characters are already effective around level 70-80, so you can omit to level them up all the way to 90.
